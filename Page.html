This is Audio Dentist a pioneer in Abrasive Metal genre.

Avant-garde music often fails. To excel, this bold artistic ideas are supported with internalized knowledge of advance music theory, extensive experience, and flawless production. Abrasive Metal is a conscious challenge to established norms of music theory, seamlessly incorporating polyrhythms, free rhythm, improvisation, sound design, polychords, atonality, and daring key changes. This genre is relying on improvisation to create chaotic environment for heavily distorted guitars and frequent use of hard dissonance to emphasize the tension before resolving down creating satisfying release. The result is an innovative sonic tapestry that defies conventions and breaks new ground. Described as "abrasive", "intense", and "powerful" his compositions channel a chaotic energy that leaves an indelible mark.

Witness the chaotic power of a mind unbound by tradition, and experience a sonic journey like no other.
